Our Mission & Vision

Learn about our mission, vision, values, and mandate

Our Mission

To ensure efficient and effective managing and reporting of Government’s financial operations, in order to support and foster the achievements of the Government’s goals and objectives with the highest level of proficiency, confidentiality and professionalism.

Our Vision

"Collecting all Government Revenues due, and ensuring they are spent only on Public Services"

Our Values
  • Transparency
  • Professionalism
  • Confidentiality

Our Mandate

The Accountant General's Department is administered under the Finance Administration Act, 2007. In accordance with the directions of the Financial Secretary, the Accountant General is responsible for:

  • 1 Maintaining central accounts of the Government showing the current state of the Consolidated Fund
  • 2 Receiving, banking and overseeing disbursement of public money
  • 3 Preparing Public Accounts and financial statements
  • 4 Maintaining a system for examination of payments
  • 5 Ensuring proper system of accounts in every ministry and department
  • 6 Evaluating accounting and financial management systems throughout Government
  • 7 Reporting defects in control of revenue, expenditure or cash
  • 8 Exercising supervision over revenue receipt and collection
