Pension Services

The Treasury processes pension payments for retired public servants and administers the Social Security pension program.

Pension Calculator

Calculate your estimated government pension and gratuity based on years of service and final salary

Eligibility Requirements:
  • Minimum 10 years (120 months) of service for gratuity
  • Minimum 15 years (180 months) of service for pension eligibility
  • Maximum pensionable service 33⅓ years (400 months)
  • 3% contribution rate for contributory pension plan
Your annual salary at the time of retirement
How It's Calculated:
  • Annual Pension: (Service in months ÷ 600) × Final Annual Salary
  • Monthly Pension: Annual Pension ÷ 12
  • Gratuity: (Annual Pension × 4) + 12.5
Maximum pensionable service is capped at 400 months (33⅓ years).

Payment Schedule:

Life Certificates:
Pensioners are required to submit life certificates annually to continue receiving payments.
