Annual National Budget

The Treasury Department, under the Ministry of Finance, prepares the annual budget in accordance with the Public Finance Management Act. The budget outlines the expected revenue, expenditure priorities, investments, and fiscal responsibilities of the Government.

Budget Overview

The national budget sets out the Government's spending plan and priorities for the fiscal year.

Key Budget Themes

  • Supporting economic growth
  • Public infrastructure development
  • Fiscal responsibility and transparency
