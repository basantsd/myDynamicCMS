Customer Service Unit

Central Revenue Collection Agency for Government Services

The Customer Service Unit serves as the central revenue collection agency for various government ministries and departments. We facilitate efficient and secure payment processing for a wide range of government services.

Services We Collect For:
  • Business licenses and permits
  • Government fees and charges
  • Trade licenses
  • Property taxes
  • Motor vehicle registrations
  • Court fines and fees
Accepted Payment Methods:
Cash
Debit Card
Credit Card
Bank Draft
Certified Check
Wire Transfer
